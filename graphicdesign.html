MAGAZINE COVERS

The magazine cover illustrations for Connect
Magazine by the Indian Institute of Science
explores the phenomenon of gravitational lensing,
depicting the warping effect of massive objects
on light in space.

Another illustration showcases the rich biodiversity
of the grasslands in Kalangal, Coimbatore, for the
Journal of Threatened Taxa by Zoo Outreach
Organization , highlighting the region's ecological
significance amidst urban encroachment.

THE FORGOTTEN RIVER

The Vrishabhavathi, a river in Bangalore, known today
for its pollution it carries as it flows through the city.
This narrative focuses on the stories of the river's tragic
transformation and the people living and working
close to the river.
